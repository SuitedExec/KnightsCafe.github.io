WELCOME TO KNIGHTS CAFE

About Us

At Knights Cafe, we're passionate about serving up more than just great coffee and delicious food.
We're on a mission to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere that makes you feel like you're part of the family.

Our Story

In year 1990, two friends, Rohan and Karan, decided to bring their love for good coffee and great company together. They opened Knights Cafe with a simple goal: to make every customer feel like a knight - treated with respect, care, and a dash of humor. Over the years, we've grown into a cozy little haven where people come to relax, socialize, and enjoy a cuppa (or two). Our menu is carefully crafted to cater to all tastes, from classic Indian dishes to international favorites. And, of course, our coffee is always freshly brewed, using only the fine finest beans.

Our Values

At Knights Cafe, we believe that:
Coffee is more than just a drink: it's an experience that brings people together.
